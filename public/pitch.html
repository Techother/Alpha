02 · The Solution

MKL Health: SMS-First RPM That Works on Any Phone

MKL Health meets patients where they are — through simple text messages on any phone — and gives providers a real-time dashboard, automated billing compliance, and instant alerts when patients deteriorate.

  • No app required for patientsDaily check-ins via SMS achieve 85% compliance vs. 38% for app-based programs
  • Automatic CPT code trackingThe platform tracks 99454/99457/99458 time thresholds in real time and generates monthly billing reports
  • AI-powered symptom triageClaude Haiku analyzes check-in responses and surfaces early warning signs before they become emergencies
  • One platform, all conditionsHeart failure, hypertension, COPD, diabetes — configurable per-condition alert thresholds
  • Integrated mental healthPHQ-9 and GAD-7 screenings auto-trigger monthly — generating additional billable encounters

10 · Technology

Production-Ready Stack with Security by Design

Architecture highlights

  • Zero credentials in browser bundleAll API keys (Airtable, Slack, Google, Anthropic, Twilio) are server-side only. Vercel serverless functions with Supabase JWT verification on every request.
  • HIPAA-ready infrastructureSupabase with Row Level Security — every clinician sees only their patient panel. Data encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Serverless at Vercel edgeSub-50ms global latency for the clinical dashboard. Auto-scaling — no ops team required.
  • AI-powered triage via ClaudeAnthropic Claude Haiku processes check-in responses, identifies symptom patterns, and drafts alert messages for nurse review.
  • SMS via TwilioTwo-way SMS gateway with no app download requirement. Delivery receipts logged for CMS documentation compliance.
